## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Shahnameh: Legends of Persia** is a three-episode action-adventure game bringing the epic Persian poem to life. Players experience the legendary tales of Rostam, the greatest hero of ancient Persia, across three interconnected episodes spanning over a century of conflicts, love, and tragedy.

**Unique Selling Points:**
- First major game based on Persian mythology
- Episodic structure allows manageable development and steady revenue
- AI-assisted production enables AAA quality with indie team size
- Rich narrative drawing from 1,000+ year-old epic poetry
- Combat inspired by Dark Souls meets God of War
- Stylized art direction inspired by Persian miniature paintings

**Market Position:**
- Fans of: *Hades*, *God of War*, *Assassin's Creed*, *Prince of Persia*
- Underserved market: Middle Eastern mythology in games
- Story-driven action-adventure is proven market (40M+ sales for similar titles)

## GAMEPLAY MECHANICS

### Core Combat System

**Philosophy:** Weighty, deliberate combat where every strike matters. Inspired by *Dark Souls* difficulty with *God of War* spectacle.

#### Basic Combat
- **Light Attack:** Fast, less damage, combo potential
- **Heavy Attack:** Slow, high damage, breaks guards
- **Dodge Roll:** I-frames for skilled play
- **Perfect Parry:** Timing-based counter that opens enemies
- **Guard:** Reduces damage, depletes stamina
- **Special Moves:** Unlockable abilities using stamina

#### Weapons
**Rostam's Arsenal:**
- **Gurz (Mace):** Primary weapon, legendary ox-headed club
  - Heavy, crushing damage
  - Effective vs armor and demons
  - Charged attacks stagger enemies
  
- **Sword:** Secondary weapon for faster combat
  - Quick combos
  - Better for human enemies
  - Parrying tool

- **Bow:** Ranged option
  - Limited arrows (tactical use)
  - Weak point targeting
  - Environmental interactions

**Sohrab's Arsenal (Episode 2):**
- Similar moveset to Rostam (genetic mirroring theme)
- Slightly faster, less powerful
- Unique special ability: "Youth's Fury" (aggressive buff)

#### Rakhsh (Horse Companion)
- Call Rakhsh in open areas
- Mounted combat
- Trampling charges
- Fast travel between discovered camps
- Emotional companion (reacts to story beats)

#### Stamina System
- All actions cost stamina
- Regenerates when not attacking/dodging
- Encourage tactical play, not button mashing
- Upgradeable stamina pool

#### Health & Recovery
- Health potions (limited, found/crafted)
- Campfire checkpoints (refill, upgrade)
- No automatic regeneration (tension maintenance)
- Simurgh's feather: One-time resurrection per episode (gift from Simurgh)

### Progression Systems

#### Character Progression
**Level Up System:**
- XP from combat and quest completion
- Level increases base stats
- Skill points for abilities

**Stats:**
- **Strength:** Damage output
- **Vitality:** Health pool
- **Endurance:** Stamina pool
- **Resilience:** Damage reduction

**Skill Trees:**
- **Warrior Path:** Combat abilities, damage, combos
- **Defender Path:** Parrying, blocking, survival
- **Hunter Path:** Bow skills, weak points, environmental

#### Equipment
- **Armor Sets:** Persian designs, stat bonuses, visual customization
- **Weapon Upgrades:** Found legendary materials upgrade damage
- **Talismans:** Equippable bonuses (speed, damage, resistance)
- **Legendary Gear:** Story rewards, unique abilities

### Exploration & World Design

#### Hub Structure
- **Episode 1:** Linear path through seven locations (trials)
- **Episode 2:** Semi-open Iran/Turan border region
- **Episode 3:** Larger semi-open world with multiple regions

#### Points of Interest
- **Campfires:** Save, upgrade, rest
- **Ruins:** Lore collectibles, background story
- **Hidden Chambers:** Optional challenge rooms with rewards
- **Shrines:** Pay respects, small bonuses
- **Merchants:** Buy potions, arrows, upgrades

#### Environmental Storytelling
- Ancient Persian ruins tell stories
- Battlefields show consequences of eternal war
- Visual progression shows passage of time
- Environmental clues foreshadow events

### Puzzle Elements (Light)
- **Environmental Puzzles:** Move objects, light fires, open paths
- **Combat Puzzles:** Defeat enemies in specific ways
- **No Traditional Puzzles:** Focus remains on combat/story
- Simurgh guidance system for stuck players

**Philosophy:** Quality over quantity, every line serves character or plot

**Dialogue Choices:**
- 2-3 options per choice moment
- Tone-based (aggressive, diplomatic, thoughtful)

- Some choices affect NPC reactions, not major plot
- Critical story beats are cinematic (maintain vision)

**Voice Direction:**
- Epic, poetic tone matching Shahnameh
- Modern pacing (not overly theatrical)
- Persian accent inspiration (not stereotypical)
- Emotional range from grief to triumph

## RISKS & MITIGATION

### Technical Risks

**Risk:** UE5 performance issues on target hardware
**Mitigation:** Early optimization focus, scalability options, dynamic resolution

**Risk:** AI tools produce inconsistent quality
**Mitigation:** Human oversight, iteration budget, hybrid approach

**Risk:** Scope creep beyond 2-person capacity
**Mitigation:** Strict scope document, regular review, episode structure allows cuts

---

### Market Risks

**Risk:** Niche subject matter limits audience
**Mitigation:** Universal themes (father-son, heroism), action-first marketing, comparisons to God of War

**Risk:** Episodic model fails to retain players
**Mitigation:** Strong cliffhangers, season pass, each episode standalone quality

**Risk:** Launch competition overshadows release
**Mitigation:** Flexible release dates, niche positioning, unique cultural angle

---

### Production Risks

**Risk:** 2-person team burns out
**Mitigation:** Realistic timeline, episode breaks allow rest, AI tools reduce grunt work

**Risk:** Cultural authenticity concerns
**Mitigation:** Consultant involvement, community feedback, respectful adaptation

**Risk:** Budget overruns
**Mitigation:** Conservative estimates, episode revenue funds next episode, minimal contractor use

## CONCLUSION

**Shahnameh: Legends of Persia** represents an achievable path to creating a meaningful, culturally significant game with a tiny team amplified by AI tools. The episodic structure allows for:

- **Rich source material** (1000-year-old epic)
- **Underserved market** (Middle Eastern mythology)

- **Proven genre** (action-adventure)

Creates a realistic path to success while telling stories that deserve to be in games.
